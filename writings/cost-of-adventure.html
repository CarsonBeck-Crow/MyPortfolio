The Cost of Adventure

By Carson Beck

In the fractured realm of Losthia, where the ruins of 1980’s Earth collided with the magical world of the Lost Lands, the Age of Adventure has faded into legend. Monsters no longer roam the wilds, and heroes have traded their swords for mundane lives. But beneath the surface, shadows stir, and the fragile peace of this strange world is about to shatter.

Tarek Qalzir is a customer service agent for the Sin Corporation, a sprawling, omnipresent megacorp that dominates every corner of Losthia. Though his job pays the bills, Tarek dreams of becoming an adventurer. But when a chance encounter with Princess Helesta thrusts him into a deadly battle with a Skeletal Lord, Tarek’s life takes a dramatic turn. Alongside a ragtag group of companions—Korrin, a brash and unconventional wizard; Alfred, a gentle healer grappling with his past; and Damien, a socially anxious but fiercely loyal protector—Tarek is drawn into a quest that will challenge everything he believes about heroism.

As Tarek and his companions navigate a world of shifting loyalties and hidden dangers, they must confront their own fears, ambitions, and the cost of their choices. The true meaning of heroism will be tested—and the cost of adventure may be higher than any of them imagined.
